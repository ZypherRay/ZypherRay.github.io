英语一小作文

注意

  1. 语言简练
  2. 要素齐全【问候语、感谢/道歉、时间、地点、目的、事情内容、结束语】
  3. 尽量使用短句
  4. 多用感谢语！感谢！感谢！
  5. 书信使用人称代词作主语
  6. 招聘和通知使用物做主语

要求

  1. 格式正确

文体

  1. 私人书信【人做主语】

  2. 正式书信【人做主语，表达正式】

  3. 招聘【物做主语，多用被动】

  4. 通知【物做主语，多用被动】

    • 注意格式

      Notice
      ​ January 9,2010【右对齐】
--------------------正文------------------
​ Postgraduate Association【右对齐】

常用表达

人称

  • Dear Sir or Madam, 【不知男女】

自谦

  • We are greatly honored to …
  • I am extremely honored to accept your invitation to ….
  • It is my great honor to…

感谢

  1. 开头
  • I am very grateful to ….
  • I appreciated the opportunity of …【较正式】
  • I just want to write a letter to you to thank you for…
  1. 结尾
  • please give my best regards to your family.【私人书信】
  • My appreciation to your generous help is beyond words.
  • your help is greatly appreciated.【投诉信感谢】

道歉

  • please accept my sincere apologies for any inconveniences …may cause.
  • Please excuse me for ….
  • I am sorry to say that I will therefore not be able to ….【用于文中】

表示难忘回忆【都能用】

  • The experience I had here are ones I will not forget for my entire life.
  • Indeed,the program is one of the most wonderful memories in my life.
  • the time you spend on your campus will be worthwhile and enhance your whole life.

邀请【邀请两遍，开头&结尾】

  • On behalf of the ….
  • I am writing to invite you to be our new judge.【开头】
  • Once again, we sincerely hope you can be the judge for our competition.【结尾】

欢迎

  • I would like to extend our heartfelt welcome to ….

建议

  • My suggestion is that the …
  • It is my great pleasure to offer you some suggestions.
  • In the first place….In the second place….
  • I would like either to ….or….

期待回复

  • Call 87654321 or email postgr@ccc.edu.cn for an application and for information on the interview.【招聘】
  • Please RSVP before this weekend.
  • I am looking forward to hearing from you soon.
  • I am looking forward to your reply.
  • Your prompt attention to my suggestion would be highly appreciated.

祝福

  • We wish you enjoy your stay in our city.
  • Hope you like it here.

结尾

Yours sincerely, 【有逗号】

Zhang Wei 【无逗号】

辩论赛主题

  • 名词——A Blessing or a Curse

学生活动

  1. 活动场所
  • Students’ Recreation Center
  • Students’ Auditorium
  1. 活动内容
  • Foreign Culture Festival
  • sports meets
  • speech contest
  • social gatherings
